1. Is a yearly subscription possible?

CertifyMe follows a monthly subscription plan. However, we can design a yearly plan on special request Talk to our team!

2. Is there any additional fee other than the mentioned amounts under the plans?

No. The setup or maintenance cost is covered by the plan benefits. However, you may need to pay extra for additional features.

3. Can I extend the yearly plan?

Yes. You can extend the existing plan anytime.

4. Do Non-profits get discounts?

Yes. Registered non-profits receive a certain amount of discount on their certification plans.

5. Is bulk credentialing possible?

Yes. Manage the awardee information in an excel sheet to maintain a seamless credentialing process. You can upload unlimited credentials in one go. All our plans are flexible based on the recipient count.
